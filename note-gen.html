Using the Note Generator:

Important:

If you click the copy button and get a pop up asking "Do you want to allow this webpage to access your clipboard?" Click yes, or else you won't be able to copy the data.

When typing the procedure number, Just type the procedures digits/letters and underscore's will be generated automatically. FOR EXAMPLE: 00.UV.02 WOULD BE ENTERED AS 00UV02.

Don't type the loan number in the short Desctiption, this will be added automatically.

Do not manually type in "HLHD - Empower - Mortgage -" for the short description, this will be generated automatically.

Always reset the form after each call. This prevents printing the wrong data.

The purpose of the Note Generator is to help create a perfect ticket every time!

Don't worry, it's simple to use... Follow along with the questionnaire. Based off of your selections, additional questions may appear. When you generate enough information for the ticket, 3 buttons will pop up.

will copy the information you should paste into the service now short description field to your clipboard.

will copy the information you should paste into the additional comments section in service now to your clipboard.

will clear the form for the next call.

The keyboard shortcut to paste is ctrl+v

Click anywhere to start...
